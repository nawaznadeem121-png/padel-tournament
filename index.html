About Padel Hub

Padel Hub was designed for enthusiasts who want professional-grade tournament organization without the overhead of complex software.

Inspired by world-class clubs, our platform offers a mobile-first experience that works perfectly on the court. Whether you are running a 12-team social mix or a competitive club ranking, we provide the tools to make every match count.

Official Rules & Regulations

01. Scoring System

Matches follow standard tennis scoring (15, 30, 40, Game). In our tournament manager, you input the set scores directly.

02. The Serve

Serves must be underhand and struck at or below waist level. The ball must bounce in the opponent's service box.

03. The Walls

The ball must bounce on the ground before hitting any wall or fence to be considered "in".

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my tournament history?

In the Tournament > History section, use the "Export DB" button to save a file to your phone/PC. You can "Import" this later if you clear your browser cache.

How many teams can I host?

Our current setup is optimized for a professional 12-team bracket with 3 groups of 4.
